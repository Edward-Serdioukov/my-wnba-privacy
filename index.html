Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

1. Data Collection

My WNBA Players does NOT collect personal data. No accounts, emails, or identifiers stored.

2. Third-Party Services

App fetches public sports data from:

  • site.api.espn.com — player stats & schedules
  • site.web.api.espn.com — search & game logs

No user data sent to ESPN. Only anonymous API requests for stats.

3. Data Usage

  • ✅ App Functionality only
  • ❌ No tracking
  • ❌ No analytics
  • ❌ No ads
  • ❌ No sharing

4. Local Storage

The Application stores your search history, favorite players, and settings locally on your device using Web Storage (localStorage). This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains under your exclusive control. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the application cache or uninstalling the app.
